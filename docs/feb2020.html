Cryptography essentials

Does only mission critical application need security? Ever thought that you must be a security expert to find security holes in your application? 'AES', 'RSA', 'HMAC', 'ECDSA' sound more confusing than helpful? 'Encoding', 'encryption' and 'hashing' sound familiar, but then not so much? In this talk, we will be exploring on applying the security mindset to day-to-day testing with the constructs of cryptography under your toolbelt.
