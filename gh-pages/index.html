VodQA, also called Value Oriented Discussion on Quality Analysis is a forum that sports the moto - 'Come Learn Something New'. The forum offers a unique platform within the software testing industry to strengthen the QA community through knowledge sharing.

This edition of VodQa will give you the 'TASTE OF MOBILE'. We will discuss new practices and ideas in the field of mobile testing. If you have come across any story or aspect of mobile testing that caught your interest and you would like to share it with the wider audience or if you have worked on a tool that could galvanize the testing community, then this is the right platform for you. You could present your thoughts as a hands-on workshop, a short lightning talk (\~10 mins) or a detailed presentation (\~30 mins).

We love feedback! If you have any suggestions or cribs, feel free to fill out our feedback form. Don't worry, its completely anonymous.
