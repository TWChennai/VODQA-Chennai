'vodQA' - Value Oriented Discussion on Quality Analysis with moto 'Come Learn Something New' offers a unique platform in software testing industry to strengthen the QA community by sharing and learning.

If you have come across any topic that kindled your interest and would like to share with a wider audience or if you worked on a tool that could galvanize the testing community, then this is the right platform for you to present as hands-on workshop, short lightning talk or detailed presentation.

We love feedback! If you have any suggestions or cribs, feel free to fill out our feedback form. Don't worry, its completely anonymous.
